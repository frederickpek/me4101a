Graph Creation Features

  1. Left-click to create vertices.
  2. Left-click on vertices to create edges, left-click on some other vertex to end the edge creation.
  3. Right-click on vertices and edges to delete them.
  4. Use the scoller button to drag and drop vertices. (sorry laptop users)
  5. Press Ctrl + Z to undo. (yes, Ctrl, even on mac)
  6. Press Ctrl + Y to redo.
  7. Click on edge-weights to edit their values, press enter or click in the gray area to confirm.

Graph Algorithm Simulation Features

  1. Click "Run" under the Algorithms panel to visualise any of the graph algorithms. The default source vertex is 0.
  2. Play/pause the animation.
  3. Click the [<<] and [>>] buttons to play the previous and next animation respectively.
  4. Click the [|<] and [>|] buttons to jump to the start and end of the animation respectively.
  5. While the animation is paused, drag the progress bar to skip to the animation is any time.
